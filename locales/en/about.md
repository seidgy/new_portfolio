# WHY I STOPPED BUILDING JUST INTERFACES.

Over time, I realized that code and design alone do not solve real business problems. If the wrong product is built, the best engineering in the world won't save it.

## That's why I learned research.
To understand the real pain points of users before drawing any screens.

## That's why I facilitate workshops.
To align technology, business, and design in the same direction, co-creating integrated solutions.

## That's why I still write code.
Because ideas without execution have no value. I keep building to ensure that the design vision becomes a reality with the highest technical quality.
